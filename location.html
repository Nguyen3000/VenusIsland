Location

Cat Ba Island covering an area of 323km2, is the largest and most diversified island of Ha Long Bay islands. It includes the national Park and World biosphere Reserves, with extraordinarily beautiful white sand-banks associated with large and small islands that constitute a miracurous picture.

Cat Ba island is the gateway of the largest commercial port of the part of North Vietnam and Southwest of China (the Government has just had a highway from Lao Cai to Cat Hai island and 200-250 million tons per year Lach Huyen deep-water Port built). Cat Ba is 30km far from Hai Phong City, 20km from Ha Long, and 120km from Hanoi.
